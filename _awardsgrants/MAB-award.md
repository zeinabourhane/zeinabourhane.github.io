I have been awarded the UNESCO Man and the Biosphere (MAB) award for my project, “Elaboration of the First Microbial Study of the Environmental Quality of the Jabal Moussa Biosphere Reserve, in Lebanon”. This award aims to promote new generations of scientists poised to address ecological and sustainability issues worldwide and encourage them to work on ecosystems, natural resources and biodiversity preservation in support of the Sustainable Development Goals.
